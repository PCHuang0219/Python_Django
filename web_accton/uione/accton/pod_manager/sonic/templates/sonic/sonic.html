Why SONiC?

Container Based Modular Design

Container design allows SONiC to offer less overhead, increase portability, and more consistent operation. Containers allow applications to be more rapidly deployed, patched or scaled.

Accelerates Software Evolution

SONiC’s containerized components approach makes rapid prototyping, agile development possible. It’s evolving fast and moving swiftly. It accelerates development, test and production cycles.

Free Choice of Hardware
(chipsets that support SAI)

SONiC is built on SAI. Switch Abstraction Interface is a thin software layer sits on top of hardware platform. It allows hardware vendors to fully explore their potential and deliver the best performance equipment they can make.

It’s Free and Growing Rapidly

SONiC has gained wide industry support during the recent years that includes major network chip vendors like Broadcom, Marvell and Cavium. It currently has over 40 partners including Facebook and Edgecore and the list is growing.
