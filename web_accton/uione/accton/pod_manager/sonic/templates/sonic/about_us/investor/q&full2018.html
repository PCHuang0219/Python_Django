Fourth Quarter and Full Year 2018 Results (Consolidated)

HSINCHU, Taiwan, March 21, 2019 â€“ Accton Technology Corporation (Accton) (TWSE: 2345), a premier provider of communication solutions, today reported the financial results for its fourth quarter and year ended December 31, 2018. The consolidated sales revenue for fourth quarter was NT$13.03 billion, an increase of 27.87% year-on-year, and net income was NT$799 million, and NT$1.44 per share.
