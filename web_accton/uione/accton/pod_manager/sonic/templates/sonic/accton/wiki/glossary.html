Glossary

FRU
FRU is field replaceable unit. A Edgecore switch for example can be one FRU, same with power supplies or linecards or fan trays or optical modules. It is kind of similar to SKU.
DVT
DVT is device verification test, sometimes called evt or other #VT . This is for R&D to bringup and test the stability of the hardware and software
I&V
I&V is integration and verification test. Usually R&D is not involved here and some system or integrator testers see if the unit works in the network setup.
