Use Cases

Enterprise Wi-Fi

IngniteNet’s Cloud-managed Wi-Fi solutions are a great choice for enterprise Wi-Fi deployments. Low cost, easy installation, and intuitive configuration UIs are perfect for small SOHO networks. The more advanced features make sense for large multi-site corporations requiring flexible management and access to remote networks from anywhere in the world. Multiple access point models also ensure there is a device to fill the needs of any size or type of deployment. 802.11ac Wave2 technology makes it possible to support large numbers of concurrent subscribers and caters to the needs of the most resource demanding applications.

Hotspot networks

IgniteNet Wi-Fi is a great option for building hotspot networks both indoor and outdoor. Universal access points with IP-55 rated enclosures (Spark W2 and SunSpot W2) simplify logistics and deployment as the same model can operate in indoor and outdoor environments. Multiple external captive portal applications are available as easy-to-setup add-ons from the Cloud-controller (Black BX, Cloud4Wi, for example). These add-ons allow you to cater the needs of different industries including retail, hospitality, and many more. Additionally, IgniteNet MetroLinq products are a great compliment for building reliable multi-gigabit backhaul for your hotspot networks.

Hospitality

A stable internet connection is an essential part of a customer’s experience in the hospitality market, no matter if that location is a small bed and breakfast, or a large worldwide hotel chain providing conference and event-hosting services. Great performance and low total cost of ownership make the IgniteNet Cloud-managed Wi-Fi solution ideal for this market. Centralized management and monitoring also allows easy expansion and simplifies monitoring and remote troubleshooting. This decreases OPEXs, shortens the troubleshooting time, and increases customer satisfaction. Multiple applications are also available via the IgniteNet Cloud-controller marketplace to compliment basic connectivity. These applications offer services for comprehensive Wi-Fi analytics, various Wi-Fi marketing options, and high-end security features. All of these options create additional value and improve customer satisfaction rates.

Education

Today’s education system requires robust Wi-Fi connectivity in order to cater the needs of resource-demanding applications, such as video streaming in a classroom to many simultaneously connected student devices, serving a high number of users during breaks and events, and ensuring the security of the network. IgniteNet’s Wi-Fi products paired with the cloud-based controller are a perfect fit for building networks in schools and campuses. One of IgniteNet’s marketplace applications, Familiy Zone, allows customers to run an extensive security and content filtering platform on top of IgniteNet access points. IgniteNet MetroLinq devices are also a practical option to deploy along side Wi-Fi APs for school or campus video surveillance backhaul in order to keep the 2.4 and 5 GHz frequencies open for Wi-Fi access and transmission of mission critical data.

Service provider and operator Wi-Fi

Increasing demands for high-speed connectivity mean users expect an enterprise level quality of service, even for residential applications. Solid coverage and the ability to maintain simultaneous high-speed connections to multiple devices are the key factors to satisfy these expectations. IgniteNet Wi-Fi devices check these boxes for both operators and WISPs. In addition, remote management and monitoring via the IgniteNet Cloud simplifies supervision and maintenance of these devices. The IgniteNet Add-on marketplace further extends the value for residential Wi-Fi users by enabling optional parental controls through the Family Zone application, or aiding the installation of video surveillance cameras through the SMC Secure Watch application. Low cost and great performance make the new 802.11ac Wave2 cloud-managed Wi-Fi products a perfect choice for residential deployments.

Managed service providers (MSPs)

The IgniteNet managed Wi-Fi solution gives service providers the opportunity to offer managed Wi-Fi services to enterprise customers who don’t have a dedicated IT staff. The cloud-based controller makes configuration easy, and enables monitoring and troubleshooting of remote networks from anywhere in the world. The simple licensing structure (99 USD per year for unlimited devices) makes it a hassle free solution. With IgniteNet, MSPs can enable their customers, like retail stores, offices, hotels, and others to concentrate on their main business activities while enjoying fast, secure, and reliable Wi-Fi connectivity.
