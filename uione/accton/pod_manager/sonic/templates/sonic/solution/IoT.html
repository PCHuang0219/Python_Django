Smart Light Control and Environment Surveillance Systems

Due to fast population growth, lighting and energy must strive to meet the sustainable trends in energy, water, and cost efficiency. The Ceres smart light control system is a cloud-based management system that enables remote monitoring and setting adjustment. It’s smart illuminance setting reduces power waste. Moreover, the device can be extended as an environment surveillance controller to monitor temperature, humidity, air quality, and sound. For example, the Ceres smart light control system can be used with single or multiple wireless sensors, such as sprinkler and soil moisture sensors, to automatically apply watering according to the soil moisture level, thus saving water.

IoT Accelerates the Integration of Heterogeneous Systems and Functions

Accton’s Chain Store Surveillance solution (CSS) integrates a remote Central Management System (CMS), a PoE IP camera, a Network Video Recorder (NVR), and a customized audio box in one system. This specially developed surveillance platform and CMS supports NVR with remote firmware upgrades and time synchronization. The CSS saves operating expenses as it requires no huge cloud storage, utilizes the local store network bandwidth, and centralizes the surveillance system. The pure Cloud based Video Surveillance solution (VSS) – Secure Watch is also applied as another choice.

Accton’s Smart Health Care solution is particularly designed for Nursing home and Care center. It is the first integrated solution of lighting control, smart bed and security system, combined Care Smart and Inclusive into One package.
